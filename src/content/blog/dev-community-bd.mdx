## The challenge

Local developers lacked a dedicated digital space to connect, share knowledge, and collaborate in real time. Existing platforms were too noisy or too general — the community needed a focused hub where Bangladeshi developers could network, share resources, and grow together.

## What I built

A full-stack community platform powered by Next.js with real-time database integration for instant updates. The information architecture is designed for high engagement — threaded discussions, resource sharing, member profiles, and live notifications keep the community active and connected.

## Outcomes

- Active membership grew **340%** within the first three months
- Daily posts and interactions increased **210%** as the platform gained traction
- Page loads average **0.8s** — keeping the experience snappy even during peak hours
- Community retention rate of **78%** month-over-month

> "Finally, a space built for our developer community — where conversations actually happen in real time." — Community Lead, Dev Community BD
