## The challenge

Managing shared meal expenses in group living situations was a spreadsheet nightmare. Manual calculations were error-prone, reconciliation took hours each week, and nobody had a clear picture of who owed what. The client needed a purpose-built tool that eliminated the friction.

## What I built

A lightweight React utility application powered by Context API for seamless state management. Users can log expenses, track daily operational costs, and the app automatically calculates individual shares and generates billing summaries. The clean, responsive interface works equally well on desktop and mobile.

## Outcomes

- Calculation time reduced by **92%** — from hours of spreadsheet work to seconds
- **150+** daily active users relying on the platform for expense tracking
- **100%** data accuracy with automated calculations eliminating human error
- Zero training time — the intuitive interface was adopted immediately

> "We went from arguing over spreadsheets to tapping in our expenses and getting instant answers." — Community Manager
