## The challenge

The paint industry faces a unique digital problem: customers need to browse hundreds of color variants quickly, compare finishes, and make confident purchases without seeing the product in person. The existing site struggled with slow load times and a clunky browsing experience that killed conversion.

## What I built

A full-stack WordPress storefront engineered for speed and usability. I implemented dynamic color swatches that render instantly, automated checkout flows that reduce cart abandonment, and a strict technical SEO layer that pushed the site to the top of search results for high-intent paint queries.

## Outcomes

- Page load time dropped **52%** through optimized asset delivery and caching
- Conversion rate climbed **34%** with the streamlined checkout experience
- SEO visibility reached the **top 3** positions for key commercial search terms
- Dynamic swatches reduced bounce rate by keeping visitors engaged in color exploration

> "The new storefront made browsing paint colors actually enjoyable — and our bottom line shows it." — Owner, Deluxy Paint
