## The challenge

The pet supply market in Bangladesh was underserved online. The client needed a marketplace that could handle thousands of products across dozens of categories — pet food, accessories, grooming services, and veterinary booking — all within a single platform that felt effortless for both customers and admins.

## What I built

A full-stack MERN marketplace with a streamlined multi-step checkout flow, advanced product filtering (by category, brand, price, pet type), and a comprehensive admin dashboard for inventory and order management. The platform scales from dozens to thousands of products without performance degradation.

## Outcomes

- **2,400+** products listed across multiple categories with fast search and filtering
- Order processing time reduced by **55%** through automated workflows
- Customer satisfaction rating of **4.8/5** — driven by the seamless shopping experience
- Admin team can manage inventory in minutes instead of hours

> "Our customers love browsing products, and our team finally has control over inventory." — Owner, Pet Shop BD
