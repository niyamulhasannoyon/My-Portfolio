## The challenge

The client's legacy React SPA was shipping 1.4MB of JavaScript on first paint,
causing a 4.2s LCP and a high bounce rate from cold LinkedIn traffic.

## What I built

I re-platformed the marketing site and dashboard onto the **Next.js App Router**
with server components, streaming, and a re-indexed MongoDB aggregation layer.
Edge caching and image optimization removed the heaviest render paths.

## Outcomes

- Page load dropped from 4.2s to **1.3s**
- Trial signups from paid cold traffic rose **23%**
- Lighthouse performance scored **98** across key landing pages

> "The rebuild paid for itself in the first month of ad spend." — Founder, UK SaaS
