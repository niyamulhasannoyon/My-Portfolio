## The challenge

The SaaS product was strong, but the landing page wasn't converting. Visitors landed, glanced, and left — without understanding the value proposition or taking the next step. The interface needed to communicate sophistication instantly while guiding users toward sign-up.

## What I built

A premium React landing page with Framer Motion micro-interactions that create a polished, trustworthy feel. The typographic hierarchy leads the eye naturally through the value proposition, while performance optimization ensures the experience is as fast as it is beautiful.

## Outcomes

- Sign-up conversion rate increased **47%** after the redesign
- Lighthouse score of **96** — among the highest in the SaaS landing page category
- Bounce rate dropped **31%** as micro-interactions kept visitors exploring
- Average time on page increased by **53%**

> "The new page finally communicates the quality of our product before a single line of code is tried." — Product Lead, Gorix Coral
