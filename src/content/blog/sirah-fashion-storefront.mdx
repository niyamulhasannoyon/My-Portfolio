## The challenge

Fashion retail lives and dies on visual impact. The client needed a storefront that could serve high-resolution product imagery instantly — without the sluggish load times that plague image-heavy fashion sites. Every millisecond of delay was costing engagement.

## What I built

A Next.js-powered storefront with an optimized media delivery pipeline. Images are automatically compressed, resized, and served in modern formats. Fluid animations guide the browsing experience, and the responsive layout ensures a premium feel on every device.

## Outcomes

- Lighthouse Performance score of **97** — among the fastest in fashion e-commerce
- Image load times reduced by **74%** through next-gen format serving and CDN delivery
- Mobile bounce rate dropped **28%** as the instant-loading gallery kept shoppers engaged
- Seamless product discovery flow increased average session duration by **41%**
